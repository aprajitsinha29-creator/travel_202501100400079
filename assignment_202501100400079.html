Travel Terminology

Itinerary
A planned route or journey, typically including a list of places to visit and activities to do.
Visa
An official document or stamp in a passport that allows the holder to enter, leave, or stay in a country for a specified period.
Jet Lag
A temporary sleep disorder that occurs when a person travels quickly across multiple time zones.
